Titanium Flex Suits

Combining the better of two worlds to make one remarkable wetsuit!

Harvey's TFlex Jumpsuits are our most comfortable jumpsuits and one of our warmest suits to date. The Flex Jumpsuit has been redesigned and anatomically cut to enhance your comfort and mobility. Using our Titanium lined Flex material enables our Flex Jumpsuit to stretch up to 250% its original size while the Titanium foil sheets trap and retain body heat, keeping you warmer for longer and when combined with glide skin wrist and ankle seals further enhance thermal heating properties and reducing cold water re-entry into the suit. The Flex jumpsuit is easy to don and fits like your own skin. Our nylon is nearly impervious to Velcro, weight belt or BC damage and with the added protection from the Dino-Hide® shoulder pads and knee pads.

The Flex Jumpsuit will continue to look like new for years and provide the diver with maximum warmth, comfort, and maneuverability. No matter the diving condition.
