Titanium Dino-Hide ® Cold Water Mitts

Models:
5.0mm (TNFDH 802V)

Description:
The use of TFN material with a Dino-Hide® coating makes these gloves warm and durable for work or play. Dino-Hide® is an extremely tough yet flexible coating. The TNFDH 802V model includes Velcro wrist straps to help keep a tight seal around your wrist. This also helps reduce any seepage through wrist seals on dry suits. The combination of a three finger mitt configuration combined with Velcro wrist straps TFN material make this model our warmest and most durable mitts. Three finger mitts also allow you to spear fishing and crab or bug hunting in colder climates where a glove would allow your fingers to lose too much body temperature.
