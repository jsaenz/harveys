Kobalt

Just like the old school Skin-In neoprene suits popular in the early days of scuba but with a high tech twist.

These state of the art lines of wetsuits blends Harvey's exclusive double-sided TITANIUM foil system and Harvey’s super stretchy FLEX material with the latest in Skin-In neoprene research, producing the industry's warmest and most comfortable suits ever. Kobalt Pro-Flex® neoprene is designed to fit closer to your own skin, to eliminate the flow of water through the suit.

Kobalt Pro-Flex® silky interior glide skin and 250% stretchabiltiy makes the Pro-flex one of our easiest suits to slip on and off. The supple, smooth skin interior provides the diver with an ultra fast drying time and warm, dry repetitive dives

The Bio-Guard® system incorporated in the Kobalt interior inhibits bacterial growth for a cleaner, more hygienic suit. The Skin-In Kobalt Pro-Flex material keeps the material so close to your skin the diver remains virtually dry with little to no water flow through the suit.
